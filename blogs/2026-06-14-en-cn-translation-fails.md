A user running the LibreTranslate Docker image on a self-hosted Discourse instance reports that English-to-Chinese translation fails for a Chinese-locale user with an error that translators cannot translate en content into zh_CN, while Chinese-to-English works fine. They suspect a mismatch between Argos Translate's zh_Hans/zh_Hant models and Discourse's zh_CN locale designation.
